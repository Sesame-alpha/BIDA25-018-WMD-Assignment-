🐾 Our Story

Royal Paws Veterinary Clinic was founded in Gaborone with a simple belief: every pet deserves compassionate, high‑quality care. What started as a small mobile clinic has grown into a trusted animal hospital, blending modern medicine with a warm, personal touch.

Over the past decade, we’ve served thousands of happy pets – from playful puppies to wise senior cats. Our team of dedicated veterinarians and nurses continues to learn, using the latest techniques to keep your furry family members healthy.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, fear‑free veterinary care that enhances the lives of pets and peace of mind for their owners. We combine expertise with empathy, ensuring every visit is stress‑free and transparent.

Our Vision

To be Botswana’s most trusted veterinary partner – where innovation meets tradition, and every pet is treated like royalty. We envision a future where no pet goes without quality healthcare.

📜 Our Journey

Founded by Dr.Leatile Dithupe in 2021, Royal Paws started as a one‑person practice. Today we have six vets, a modern surgical suite, and an in‑house laboratory. We are proudly locally owned and deeply connected to the Gaborone community – sponsoring pet adoption events and offering free wellness talks in local schools.

We believe in transparent pricing, open communication, and treating every patient as if they were our own. #RoyalCareForRoyalPets
